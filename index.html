Who We Serve

Trust & Estate Attorneys

Professionals who draft wills, trusts, and powers of attorney — but often lack secure, standardized tools for helping clients manage and transfer self-custodied digital assets.

Professional Fiduciaries

Individuals or firms legally appointed to manage another person’s assets — typically through a trust, court order, or legal agreement — and tasked with acting in their client’s best interest.

Executors & Estate Administrators

Appointees named in a will or by a court to handle posthumous asset access, distribution, and recordkeeping — now facing the challenge of locating, unlocking, and transferring digital assets securely.

Corporate Trustees

Bank or law firm–based trust departments that manage complex estates or high-value holdings — increasingly including digital assets with no built-in continuity protocols.

How It Works

1. Create Your Backup

Launch the EncryptFire app on any desktop or laptop — no installation, no internet required. Generate your offline backup in minutes.

2. Split & Protect

Break your backup into secure, password-protected pieces. Store them across multiple USB drives, with optional timed cloud access if needed.

3. Enable Continuity

Use our structured handoff process to prepare digital assets for the future — whether for personal peace of mind or to support clients and beneficiaries.

About Us

Crypto Security Tools is a private, independent company focused on digital asset continuity for the self-custody era.

Our mission is to equip individuals, advisors, and legal professionals with secure, offline-first tools for managing and transferring digital assets — including seed phrases, private keys, encrypted files, and credentials while minimizing risks associated with cloud systems, custodians, or third-party intermediaries.

Built around privacy-first principles, our solutions are designed to uphold client control while enabling long-term continuity. We serve professionals navigating the legal, fiduciary, and logistical challenges of decentralized wealth — with tools that work on their terms.
